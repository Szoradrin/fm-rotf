# Cult of Auril — Journal Plan
## Source: The Creed of Auril by Samuel Dillon (third-party PDF, DMs Guild)
## Integration: One DM-facing journal entry per town, plus a master Cult of Auril overview journal
## Folder structure in Foundry: "Cult of Auril" compendium folder

---

## Journal Entries to Build

### 1. Cult of Auril — Overview
- Full faction summary: 4 tenets (Cruelty, Endurance, Isolation, Preservation)
- Rank structure table: Spite / Tempest / Darkhand / Coldwind / Icebringer / Nix Mater
- The Three Nix Mater: Fury, Anger, Wrath — shadow leadership above everything
- Greetings/passwords: "The frigid veil persists" / "All the world in winter's white" / "May her blessing chill you"
- How the cult reframes the sacrifice narrative: not desperate townsfolk — engineered compliance
- Actor references: link to each rank's stat block in rotf-actors-cult

### 2. Bremen — Cult Presence
- Coldwind: Allosa Faerwill (1)
- Darkhands: Harklen and Lerro (2)
- ~15 cultists total
- Knows about black ice amulets carried by Knights of the Black Sword
- Watching anyone who asks about the white moose / Ravisin connection
- Thread: if party exposes black ice link, Allosa moves to protect cult interests

### 3. Bryn Shander — Cult Presence
- Icebringer: Jelenneth Arnuanna — has ordered 50 cultists to go dark
- Cult is LOSING here — Markham Southwell + priestess Mishann of Amaunator actively delegitimizing Auril worship
- Safehouse: across from Blackiron Blades
- Thread: if Markham dies or is weakened, Jelenneth moves immediately
- Stakes: largest town, biggest prize, closest to tipping

### 4. Caer-Dineval — Cult Presence
- Coldwind: Timmis Welbe — charming, will befriend party, most dangerous cult member in the Dale
- Cult knows about Knights of the Black Sword black ice — planning infiltration
- If party ousts duergar from Dinev's Rest, cult moves in
- Thread: Timmis will try to play party against the Knights

### 5. Caer-Konig — Cult Presence
- Coldwind: Sariel Myra
- Darkhands: Seebo Raulnor, Burgess, Pelmo
- ~22 cultists (14% of population — highest density in Dale)
- Shorard sisters may be Tempests
- Frozenfar Expeditions dog breeder supplies cult sleds
- Thread: if party needed sleds and something went wrong — now they know why

### 6. Dougan's Hole — Cult Presence (ACTIVE — NEXT SESSION)
- Icebringer: Savaa Rubyeye — stat block: Icebringer of Auril (CR 6)
- Openly using Twenty Stones of Thruun as ice temple
- Planning to officiate Midwinter sacrifice ceremony
- Speaker Edgra Durmoot resisting — has stopped attending Council of Speakers
- Thread: party walks into active power struggle — first time cult has a visible face
- This is the entry point. Everything before was rumors. Everything after is consequence.

### 7. Easthaven — Cult Presence
- Coldwind: Jia Inbar — deepest roots in the Dale, cult HQ
- Darkhands: Makkis Lecler, Sitre Xin, Veitel Patrin, Enidda Eliam, Morgan Kane, Ketha Nesh (6 darkhands)
- ~64-76 cultists
- Jia got the Easthaven ferry shut down
- Jia had Dzaan arrested
- Jia has Speaker Danneth Waylen's ear
- Cult HQ: large farmhouse just off Eastway on edge of town
- Thread: party has already felt Jia's work without knowing her name — retroactive horror payoff

### 8. Good Mead — Cult Presence
- Almost none
- If party raided Verbeeg lair: bones and scraps of cult gear — Spites who became dinner
- Small detail, easy to miss, horrifying in retrospect

### 9. Lonelywood — Cult Presence
- Ravisin: loner, no rank, no coordination with hierarchy
- Will not attack anyone wearing Auril's symbol
- Thread: if party spared/befriended Ravisin, she's a loose thread connecting to coldwind network

### 10. Targos — Cult Presence
- Wild card: Naerth Maxildanarr is Zhentarim — cult wants him flipped or neutralized
- Option A: Naerth stays Zhent, cult infiltrating around him
- Option B: Naerth gets flipped to cult before party deals with him — confrontation completely different
- DM choice — note both options
- Thread: ticking clock the party doesn't know about

### 11. Termalaine — Cult Presence
- Vein of black ice chardalyn deep in gem mine
- Cult knows it's there, wants it
- Kobolds are a convenient distraction
- Thread: cult has been trying to gain control of the mine

## Nix Mater — The Shadow Above Everything
- Three snow mothers: Fury, Anger, Wrath
- Known only to coldwinds and above
- Slowly transforming into hag-like crones
- Move between locations, appear at ceremonies
- Party will never meet one until the campaign is ready
- Stat block: Nix Mater (CR 9) in rotf-actors-cult
- Their existence reframes every cult encounter retroactively
